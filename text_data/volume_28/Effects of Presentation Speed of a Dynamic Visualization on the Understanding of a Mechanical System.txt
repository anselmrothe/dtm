                                                                                       Hypotheses
                                                                 On the level of attentional processes, it was expected
                                                                 that different presentation speeds would affect distribu-
                                                                 tion of attention and lead to different attentional pro-
                                                                 files. Indicators for attention should be found in verbal
                                                                 reports and pointing behaviour during animation per-
                                                                 ception. On the level of comprehension, improved men-
                                                                 tal model building and understanding was expected, if
                                                                 a changed presentation speed increases attention to the-
                                                                 matically relevant components. For the pendulum clock
                                                                 this should be achieved by increasing attention to the
                                                                 key components of the drive mechanism (i.e. pendulum,
                                                                 escapement and particularly the weight).
