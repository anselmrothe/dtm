           visualInputÅ read(environment)
           handObjectFeaturesÅ AIPComputation(visualInput, task)

                               objectLoacationÅ where(visualInput)

                     ENDWHILE
           ELSE
                     WHILE c is not NIL DO
                               evÅ EPGenerator(v,c)
